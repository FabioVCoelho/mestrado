| Acronym                    | Full Meaning (Portuguese)                     | English Translation                | Explanation                                                                                                |
| -------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **DTA (mm/m)**             | Disponibilidade Total de Água                 | Total Available Water (TAW)        | Maximum volume of water that can be stored in the soil between field capacity and permanent wilting point. |
| **CTA (mm)**               | Capacidade Total de Água                      | Total Water Capacity               | Represents total soil water storage up to a given depth. Sometimes synonymous with TAW.                    |
| **CRA (mm)**               | Capacidade de Retenção de Água                | Water Retention Capacity           | The actual water retained in the soil after drainage (field capacity).                                     |
| **LA (mm)**                | Lâmina de Água                                | Water Layer or Water Depth         | Volume of water available in a given soil layer; derived from volumetric moisture and depth.               |
| **LC (mm)**                | Lâmina Crítica                                | Critical Water Layer               | Threshold of soil water below which crops start experiencing stress.                                       |
| **(LA-LC)**                | Diferença entre Lâmina Atual e Lâmina Crítica | Water Deficit Indicator            | If negative, it indicates **water stress**; if positive, no stress.                                        |
| **CC (m³/m³)**             | Capacidade de Campo                           | Field Capacity                     | Volumetric moisture when the soil has drained after saturation and holds maximum available water.          |
| **PMP (m³/m³)**            | Ponto de Murcha Permanente                    | Permanent Wilting Point            | Minimum soil moisture at which plants can no longer extract water.                                         |
| **AD**                     | Água Disponível                               | Available Water                    | Fraction of soil water between CC and PMP that plants can use.                                             |
| **PD**                     | Ponto de Drenagem                             | Drainage Point                     | Sometimes used to represent water content right after rainfall or irrigation, before drainage reaches CC.  |
| **DTA (mm/m)**             | Disponibilidade Total de Água                 | Total Available Water              | Repeats for each layer, 0–30, 30–60, etc.                                                                  |
| **CTA 0–60cm (mm)**        | Capacidade Total de Água até 60cm             | Total Water Capacity (0–60 cm)     | Combines CTA for multiple layers.                                                                          |
| **CRA 0–60cm (mm)**        | Capacidade de Retenção de Água até 60cm       | Water Retention Capacity (0–60 cm) | Sum of retention capacities up to 60 cm.                                                                   |
| **LA–LC**                  | Diferença entre Lâmina Atual e Crítica        | Water Stress Indicator             | Used in conditional formula: if (LA–LC) < 0 → stress.                                                      |
| **“(LA–LC)<0 = estresse”** | Regra de Estresse Hídrico                     | Hydric Stress Condition            | The logical statement indicating stress if LA–LC < 0.                                                      |


| Variable                                 | What It Represents                                                                                            | Why It Might Be Constant                                                                                                                             |
| ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **DTA (Disponibilidade Total de Água)**  | The total volume of water the soil *can* store between field capacity (CC) and permanent wilting point (PMP). | Depends **only on soil texture, structure, and depth**, not on daily conditions — so if your soil profile and depth are fixed, DTA remains constant. |
| **CTA (Capacidade Total de Água)**       | The total storage capacity of the soil for a given layer (physical property).                                 | Also fixed by soil physical characteristics — unless you change depth or soil type.                                                                  |
| **CRA (Capacidade de Retenção de Água)** | The retained water at field capacity (CC).                                                                    | Field capacity is a **constant property** of each soil type, so CRA doesn’t vary unless the soil or depth interval changes.                          |
